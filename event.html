NOOSE TROUBLE

NOOSE TROUBLE

About the event-

It is not as simple as it looks! Noose-Trouble is basically wire loop game having the metal loop that has to be taken from intial point to the final point along the length of wire. As we have already told it's not that easy. So here is the something that will take it go up to another level, the metal loop should not touch wire at any point. If the metal loop touches the wire then the game will be lost by first player and further it should be continued by another player.
You need both pace and patience for this!
